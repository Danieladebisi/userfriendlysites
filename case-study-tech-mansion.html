Tech Mansion — Credibility, clarity, and conversions

We redesigned Tech Mansion's site to clearly articulate value, showcase services, and drive more qualified inquiries.

Client: Tech Mansion Industry: Technology / Services Services: UX, Web Design, Development, SEO Timeline: 3 weeks

Overview

Tech Mansion wanted a website that reflected their expertise and converted visitors into leads. The old layout buried key proof and made it hard to contact the team.

The Challenge

  • Unclear information hierarchy and weak visual credibility.
  • Slow pages and unoptimized media affected SEO and engagement.
  • Contact and booking actions were inconsistent across pages.

Strategy

  • Refocus the message around outcomes and priority services.
  • Introduce social proof, case highlights, and trust indicators.
  • Streamline CTAs and forms for a consistent lead flow.

Execution

We delivered a fast, polished site with strong typography, modular blocks, and clear CTAs. We optimized images, improved semantics and accessibility, and tuned performance for core web vitals.
