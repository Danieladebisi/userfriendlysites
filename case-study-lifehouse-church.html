Lifehouse Church — Engage, inform, and grow

We rebuilt the website to help congregants quickly find service times, sermons, events, and giving — optimized for mobile.

Client: Lifehouse Church Industry: Nonprofit / Community Services: UX, Web Design, Development, CMS, SEO Timeline: 3 weeks

Overview

Lifehouse Church needed a modern, clear site to serve visitors and members. The old site buried key details like service times and events, making it harder to get involved.

The Challenge

  • Hidden essentials: service times, directions, and giving were hard to find on mobile.
  • Events and sermons lacked structure and searchability.
  • Editors needed a simpler CMS to keep content current.

Strategy

  • Redesigned IA with prominent service times and clear next steps for newcomers.
  • Structured events and sermons for quick browsing and sharing.
  • Performance-first build with accessible patterns and easy-to-manage content.

Execution

We delivered a clean, inviting design with a concise header, sticky CTAs, and clear sectioning. We improved mobile navigation, added a streamlined giving entry point, and optimized media for speed. Editors now update content in minutes.
