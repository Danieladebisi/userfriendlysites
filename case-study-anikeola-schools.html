Anikeola Schools — Clarity, speed, and trust

We partnered with Anikeola Schools to create a modern, mobile-first site that streamlines admissions inquiries and showcases academic excellence.

Client: Anikeola Schools Industry: Education Services: UX, Web Design, Development, SEO Timeline: 4 weeks

Overview

Anikeola Schools needed a refreshed digital presence to communicate their strengths—academics, community, and student outcomes—while simplifying the path to contact and admissions inquiries.

The Challenge

  • Outdated design with dense content made key information hard to find.
  • Slow load times on mobile, leading to drop-offs.
  • Admissions call-to-action was buried; parents weren’t sure where to start.

Strategy

  • Re-organize content around parent questions: academics, admissions, tuition, and campus life.
  • Introduce a clear, persistent CTA: “Book a Tour” and “Request Info.”
  • Build a performance-first, accessible frontend with image optimization and semantic structure.

Execution

We created a clean, modern layout with strong typography and intuitive navigation. We optimized images, added structured data, and improved technical SEO. A simplified admissions flow with visible CTAs now guides families to the next step.
